About Me

I have always been very interested in technology and how it shapes our world. One of favourite childhood memories is turning on a computer I built with my dad for the first time. After moving to London late in 2016, I met a lot of people working in the Web Development Industry and was convinced to try and turn that hobby and enjoyment into a career.

I attended a Web Development Bootcamp aimed at Full Stack Development at General Assembly London this January and am hoping to apply what I have learned there in practice. I have always been driven to understand my world on a conceptual level and use that understanding to solve complex problems. Web Development lets me do just that while continuously learning.

When I am not fiddling with a side project I enjoy cycling - both road and mountain biking -, squash and reading.

Star Gazer - A MEAN-Stack App

Star Gazer is an app aimed at amateur astronomers and anyone with an interest in stargazing. It uses the Nasa NeoAsteroids and some other APIs to create a list of interesting celestial events. This data is cross referenced with weather data from the Dark Sky API to provide users with a list of events that will guarantee good weather and visiblity. It also hosts a list of good locations and allows users to interact and rate the various locations..
